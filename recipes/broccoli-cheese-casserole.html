Brocolli Cheese Casserole

Description

Savory casserole made with soup and chicken and cheese


Ingredients

  • 1 Package Frozen Brocolli
  • 2 Cans Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 2 tsp. Curry Powder
  • 1 to 2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. (If skinless boneless) Thaw chicken then boil for 20 minutes or until cooked thoroughly.
  2. Let cool, then cut chicken into small chunks.
  3. Preheat oven to 350
  4. Microwave broccolli until cooked thoroughly (about 5 minutes). Drain off liquid.
  5. Mix together soup, mayo, lemon juice, curry powder. Taste and adjust lemon/curry to taste.
  6. In 9x13 pan, layer chicken, then brocolli, then mixed ingredients. Don't mix together.
  7. Top with cheddar cheese.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes.
