Cheese Enchiladas

Description

Classic cheese enchiladas with red sauce

Ingredients

  • 12 Corn Tortillas
  • 2 Cups Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Cups Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 1 Can Mild Enchilada Sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Shred cheese and mix together
  2. Heat oil to medium high
  3. Fry tortillas just to soften; put them on paper towels to blot oil.
  4. In 9x13 pan, pour a little enchilada sauce.
  5. Put tortilla in sauce and turn so sauce is on both sides.
  6. Put some cheese and roll up.
  7. Repeat the same for all of them.
  8. Pour extra sauce on top then extra cheese over top.
  9. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Serve with sour cream.
