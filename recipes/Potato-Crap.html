Potato "Crap"

Description

Classic potato casserole like "funeral potatoes."

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Hash Browns
  • 2 T. butter, melted
  • 1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 c. Milk
  • 1 c. Sour Cream
  • 1 c. Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 T. Minced Onoin (dried)
  • 1 1/2 Cups Corn Flake Crumbs
  • 1/3 Cup Parmesan Cheese (the cheap stuff)

Steps

  1. In 9 x 13 pan, layer hash browns (about 1/3 up pan--uses a whole small bag of hash browns or part of bigger bag.
  2. Melt 2 T. butter and pour over top
  3. Mix together soup, milk, sour cream, cheese, and onions (optional).
  4. Pour over top.
  5. Mix crumbs, butter and parmesan cheese together
  6. Put on top.
  7. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
